Don't study method

Don't study. It is simple, don't study and instead just get good grades. It's very simple, why worry when you can just have fun and get good grades at the same time. You can also click this page for infinite knowlege: Länk Till Kunskap

The Study Method

Study the material and make sure you know it before the tests. Instead of skipping class you can simply just be there and listen to the teacher. Make sure you study the material at home to so you are sure. This method is way more reliable than the other method.

  • Make sure to listen to the teacher
  • Study the material at home
  • Review the material before tests
  • Be calm during the test
